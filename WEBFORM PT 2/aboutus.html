About Us

Jollibee is a beloved Filipino fast-food brand, famous for its warm hospitality and tasty meals that bring people together. Whether it’s their iconic Chickenjoy, signature Jolly Spaghetti, or Yumburger, Jollibee offers comforting flavors with a distinct Filipino touch.

What began as a small ice-cream shop in the late 1970s has grown into a worldwide name — all because of their commitment to good food, fair prices, and friendly service. Today, Jollibee remains dedicated to spreading joy, sharing the spirit of Filipino hospitality, and creating memorable dining experiences for families, friends, and individuals everywhere.
